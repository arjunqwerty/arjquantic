How We Work

This is typically how a city looks like, with the light blue computers being citizens and the dark blue computers being the hospitals. The center computer is our server. The three groups of computers represent the areas in a city. For better understanding let us take just one area into account.
When you press the emergency button, all hospitals near you are notified and the hospitals have an option to either accept or decline your request, based on their availability of doctors, ambulance drivers and other necessary staffs.
When a hospital accepts your request, you are notified through the email address that you have provided that the hospital has accepted to save you. This way the whole concept of a middleman deciding what hospital to take you to is effectively eradicated.
